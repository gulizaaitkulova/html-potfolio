My top 3 movies of all time

"Interstellar"

The best sci-fi movie. I love movies about science and cosmos.

"Kabhie Khushi Kabhie Gham"

A Bollywood movie about love, family, and parent-children relationship.

"The Lion King"

The best animated movie ever. The graphics, the story, the songs - everything is great about this movie.
